About Julie

A Passion for Language & Connection

With 28 years of experience teaching Spanish and French in Rochester Community Schools — including my current role at Stoney Creek High School — I bring a deep knowledge of the district's curriculum and how local students learn best.

My interactive teaching style, often using games and activities, makes language learning both fun and effective. I strive to help students gain not just stronger skills, but also confidence and genuine enjoyment in their studies.

I tutor grades 6–12 in Spanish (levels 1 through AP) and French (levels 1 through 3), and I'm available all year — both in-person and online.
